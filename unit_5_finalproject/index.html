MOE'S TAVERN

About Drinks Music Games Events

Simpsons-themed bar with music & drinks specials every night

Moe's Tavern is more than just a bar- it is a haven for locals, musicians, Simpsons' fans, pool players, and those looking for a sense of community and family in the big city. We are all about local- locally owned, local craft beers and hosting local musicians. So stop in, and become a local!

Enjoy the following signature Moe's offerings:

  • live music five nights a week
  • drink specials every day (industry specials on Monday)
  • signature $5 cocktail of the month
  • fooseball & pool (we host tournaments as well!)
  • boardgames
  • jukebox
  • dog-friendly (come meet Lexi, our bar dog!)

send an email for any questions or to book parties!
